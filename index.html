An adventure through time

Take a journey through time! Paleora is based in the Late Cretaceous period when the last of the dinosaurs dominated the Earth. You're from the future and managed to leave your dying planet, though you weren't expecting to end up here...






Highlights

  • Encounter and interact with 15+ dinosaur species
  • Explore a beautiful, stylized 2d pixel world
  • Learn to coexist with giant, terrifying reptiles and survive in these hostile lands
  • Meet and befriend a variety of characters inhabiting various towns and settlements
  • Create your own story and decide your role in the world
