An adventure through time

Journey through Paleora as you explore Earth during the Cretaceous period, when the last of the dinosaurs ran rampant on the planet. Humankind was forced to flee the modern-day dying Earth as it rapidly approached the end of its resources. In an attempt to leave the world to find a new home, things took an unexpected turn, sending you back in time.





Highlights

  • Encounter and interact with 15+ dinosaur species
  • Explore a beautiful and lush 2d pixel world
  • Learn to coexist with giant and terrifying reptiles as you attempt to survive in new hostile lands
  • Meet and befriend a variety of characters inhabiting various towns and settlements
  • Experience your own unique story and decide what role you play in this new world as you make choices that may change your future
